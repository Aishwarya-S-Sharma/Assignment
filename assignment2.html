NAGARJUNA COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING AND TECHNOLOGY

The institution is affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University, Belagavi, recognized by the Government of Karnataka (GOK) and approved by AICTE. It is an autonomous institution with NAAC A+ accreditation.

Nagarjuna College of Engineering & Technology was established in 2001 with the basic courses of Civil, Mechanical, Electronics & Communication, Computer Science and Information Science. The college offers undergraduate program leading to 4 years Bachelors and 2 years master's degree of the Visvesvaraya Technological University in the following streams.
The college is a member of the VTU Consortium for e-resources and is recognized as Nodal Centre for virtual lab facility which is financed by Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD), Government of India (GOI).
The institution provides residential facilities for students and staff. The hostel facility have amenities like mess, recreation room, internet facility, gymnasium, automated laundry and many more.
